# Module 1: Introduction to ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) and sustainable development

## Module's Goal

This module will equip you with a fundamental understanding of ESG and its evolution, enabling you to grasp the core concepts of the Environmental, Social, and Governance pillars. It will help you see the strategic value of integrating ESG, whether you manage an SME or a large enterprise, and show how your actions link directly to global Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). This knowledge is essential for promoting sustainable development.

## Training Audience

VET providers and SMEs business managers.

## Module’s units

- Unit 1: The Three Pillars of ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance)
- Unit 2: From Corporate Philanthropy to Business Necessity
- Unit 3: Why ESG is Your Strategic Advantage
- Unit 4: Linking Your Business Actions to Global Goals

## Module's introduction

Welcome to the start of your sustainability journey. In today’s rapidly changing global economy, the terms Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) have evolved from specialised financial vocabulary to becoming the core of robust and sustainable business strategies.

Whether you are a VET provider shaping the next generation of professionals or an SME manager steering a growing company, understanding ESG is no longer just "nice to have" — it is a critical competency for survival and growth. This module serves as your roadmap. We will strip away the complexity to reveal the three core pillars of ESG, explore how the business world moved from simple charity to strategic integration, and demonstrate how your local actions resonate with the United Nations' global vision for 2030.

By the end of this module, you won't just see ESG as a checklist of rules, but as a strategic lens through which you can identify risks, discover efficiencies, and build a brand that lasts.
